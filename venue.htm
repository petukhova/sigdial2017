Venue

The conference will be held at Saarland University, Saarbrücken Campus, just outside the city of Saarbrücken, Germany.

The conference will take place in E 1.3 and E 2.2 buildings, Uni Ost - E green sector on the Interactive Campus Map

Download Saarland University App The App shows some information about studies and life at Saarland University, and offers functionality to search for busses, staff members, or browse the Mensa menu.

Travel

Air

The most two convenient airports for the conference venue are Frankfurt International Airport (FRA) and Paris International Airport (CDG), both offering non-stop connections throughout Europe as well as to North and South America, Asia and Oceania. Transportation options between the airports and USAAR are TBA.

Saarbrücken has its own Saarbrücken Airport with flights mostly operated by AirBerlin offering connections throughout Europe and to North America. For instance, flights from Berlin or Hamburg to Saarbrücken take between 1 hour 10 minutes and 1 hour 20 minutes. Saarbrücken Airport is situated about 12 km from the university campus. The bus from the airport to the university takes between 45 minutes and one hour and costs €2.50. A taxi will take you to the university in 15-20 minutes and costs around €20.

Alternatively, inexpensive flights are available to Luxembourg, Frankfurt Hahn and Düsseldorf
Luxembourg Airport (LUX) 111 km;
Frankfurt Hahn Airport (HHN) 115 km;
Düsseldorf Airport (DUS) 300 km but has convenient connecting train connections.

Rail

Saarbrücken is very conveniently reached by rail. Deutsche Bahn has a series of direct and connecting train services throughout Germany and Europe. High-speed ICE/TGV services are available from Frankfurt/Mannheim and Paris to Saarbrücken central station. Regional services run from Mainz, Trier and Strasbourg.

Car

Saarbrücken has an extensive network of roads and freeways, making it easy to get anywhere by car (if you don’t mind the traffic). However, participants probably won’t need a car for the duration of the conference, bus tickets will be included in the registration fee.

Parking Visitors can park for a fee of €1/hour (max. charge: €3; the first 60 min. are free of charge) in the campus car parks P2 ('Uni Mitte') and P3 ('Uni Ost'), see Parking at campus map. However, the parking situation at campus is rather complicated and places are limited.

Bus

Saarbrücken Campus is served by the following bus routes. There are several bus stops on campus:
- Universität Botanischer Garten (for the Science Park or the university library)
- Universität Campus (for campus zones A, B and C)
- Universität Mensa (for campus zones C, D and E, and for the research institutes INM, DFKI and MPI) - Conference venue stop!!!
- Universität Busterminal (for the research institutes IZFP and KIST)
